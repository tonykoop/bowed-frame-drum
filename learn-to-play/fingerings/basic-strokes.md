# Bowed Frame Drum — Basic Strokes & Technique

## Holding Position (Traditional Bodhran)

1. **Left arm cradle**: Grip the frame with your left hand, thumb on the outside (at 9 o'clock),
   fingers through the inside of the frame. Rest the drum against your forearm.
2. **Left hand damping**: Your left hand inside the drum controls resonance — press fingers
   against the underside of the head to damp overtones and pitch-shift the fundamental.
3. **Right hand tipper**: Hold the tipper (a short double-headed beater, ~20 cm) between thumb
   and index finger, rotating the wrist for stroke and return.

## Core Strokes (Tipper)

| Stroke | Description | Sound |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| **Tip** | Tipper tip hits center of head | Full resonant bass tone |
| **Heel** | Heel (back end) of tipper hits center | Shorter, drier accent |
| **Mute-tip** | Left hand fully damped; tip strike | Dry "thock" with no sustain |
| **Rim shot** | Tip strikes both head and rim simultaneously | Sharp crack accent |
| **Roll** | Rapid alternating tip/heel | Sustained tremolo effect |

## Tone Bar Sympathetic Shimmer

After each tip stroke, the internal tone bar (if properly tuned) adds a ~0.5–1 second
sympathetic shimmer ring. To maximize it:
- Strike firmly near head center
- Release left-hand damping after the initial attack
- Let head ring freely — shimmer builds as head oscillates

To suppress shimmer: maintain left-hand contact on head underside throughout stroke.

## Bowing Mode (Rosined Wheel)

Engage the wheel arm by pressing inward with left thumb until wheel contacts head inner rim.

**Basic bowing**:
1. Engage wheel arm
2. Apply light consistent pressure (50–100 g)
3. Rotate the drum or move the arm at 0.2–0.5 m/s
4. Allow 1–2 seconds for drone to build
5. Vary left-hand damping to shape the drone pitch (more pressure = higher pitch range)

**Drone + percussion combination**:
1. Engage drone with left thumb
2. Strike head with right-hand tipper simultaneously
3. Drone sustains under percussive attack for a hybrid drone-drum texture

## Tuning Guide (Quick Reference)

- Too low: tighten tension screws 1/4 turn each in star pattern (1-4-2-5-3-6)
- Too high: loosen 1/4 turn each
- Target fundamental: **C3 (130.8 Hz)**
- Use a chromatic tuner app — strike center of head
