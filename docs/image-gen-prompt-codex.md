# Bowed Frame Drum Image-Gen Prompts

Concept visualization only; not fabrication authority. Do not infer final
bracket geometry, wheel contact force, tone-bar dimensions, or tuning from
these prompts.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic 3/4 product photograph of a bowed frame drum concept: a bodhran-scale single-head frame drum with a matte black rolled sheet-metal cylinder frame, a natural or synthetic head, visible tension screws around the rim, and an internal flat steel tone bar suspended on small neoprene grommets. Include the optional rosined brass wheel assembly on a small pivoting arm near the inner rim, clearly presented as a real mechanism that can touch the head for sustained bowed drone. Clean studio background, a short tipper and small rosin block nearby for scale, soft directional lighting, 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic performance-workshop scene of the bowed frame drum held like a bodhran, one hand supporting the frame while the other plays with a short tipper and the thumb engages a small rosined brass wheel near the rim. Show the internal tone bar subtly through the open back, a modern metal-shop aesthetic, and a tuner or adjustment tools on a nearby bench. Natural light, believable hand posture, restrained experimental-instrument mood.

## Detail / Macro Prompt

Photorealistic macro close-up of the bowed frame drum's signature hybrid mechanism: a small brass rosined wheel on a bent steel pivot arm touching the underside edge of the drumhead, with a flat steel tone bar and neoprene grommet mount visible behind it. Emphasize real fasteners, matte black frame finish, brass edge texture, and head surface grain. High-detail macro, shallow depth of field.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, violin bow, impossible wheel contact, extra fingers, distorted hands, fake labels, or fantasy electronics.
