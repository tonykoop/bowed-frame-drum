# Image-Gen Prompts

Concept image only; not fabrication authority. Do not include cut-ready dimensions, hole locations, or toolpaths.

## Hero Prompt

A finished hybrid bowed frame drum, bodhran-scale and roughly the size of a large pizza held in one arm, photographed as a real physical object. The defining silhouette is a shallow rolled-steel cylinder frame — a single clean welded steel hoop in a matte-black powder-finish, with a thin reinforcing rim strip running around the top and bottom edges. A tan goatskin-style synthetic drum head is stretched taut across the front face and clamped over the rim by a slim silver tension wrap-ring secured with a star pattern of small cap-head screws around the circumference. Through the open back you can glimpse a thin flat steel tone bar suspended across the interior on two small dark neoprene grommets. Mounted at the inner rim, the signature feature: a small rosined brass wheel (warm gold tone) on a slender double-bent steel pivot arm, positioned so its rosined edge can touch the underside of the head to bow a sustained drone. Materials read honestly — brushed and blackened cold-rolled steel, warm brass, taut hide-toned membrane. Clean neutral light-gray studio background, soft directional studio light, three-quarter product-photography angle, ~50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic, no text labels.

## In-Context Prompt

The bowed frame drum staged in a metal-fabrication workshop, resting on a worn wood-top workbench beside a slip-roll and a TIG torch coiled in the soft background. Warm natural window light rakes across the matte-black steel frame and catches the gold of the rosined brass wheel and the taut tan head. A player's left arm begins to cradle the drum in traditional bodhran hold with a short wooden tipper resting nearby. Shallow depth of field, environmental storytelling of a shop-built hybrid percussion instrument, concept visualization only, photorealistic, no text labels.

## Detail Prompt

Macro close-up of the rosined brass wheel assembly at the inner rim of the frame drum: a small gold-toned brass wheel mounted on a stainless pivot bolt at the end of a slender double-bent blackened-steel arm, its rosined contact edge poised against the underside of the taut tan drum head near the rim. In the soft background, the thin flat steel tone bar floats on its dark neoprene grommet, isolated from the frame. Raking side light reveals brushed-metal texture, the dull sheen of rosin on brass, the weave and grain of the head membrane, and the crisp welded edge of the rim strip. High detail, shallow focus, photorealistic, no measurements.

## Negative / Avoid

No text, logos, watermarks, or fake maker marks; no dimension callouts or measurement lines; no impossible mechanisms; brass wheel and pivot arm must look load-bearing and plausible; anatomically plausible hands if a player is shown.
